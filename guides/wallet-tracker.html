üí° Why Use Wallet Trackers?

When you're farming multiple airdrop opportunities across chains, tracking your wallet activity is essential. Tools like https://zkcodex.com and https://wenser.xyz help you monitor which chains and protocols you've interacted with, how many days/weeks/months you have been active on them, how much volume you've transacted with, how many unique smart contract interactions you have and many other very important metrics.

üîç ZKCodex & Wenser

ZKCodex and Wenser both allow you to import your wallet address and check the stats for:

  • ‚Ä¢Protocols you've interacted with
  • ‚Ä¢Your relative score or positioning
  • ‚Ä¢Whether your activity meets requirements that existed for previous airdrops

How to Use:

  1. Go to https://zkcodex.com or https://wenser.xyz
  2. Connect your wallet (or copy and paste your wallet address)
  3. Select the protocol/chain that you want to see your stats for
  4. Explore the protocols and activities that they recommend for each chain, try to use as many of them as possible to increase your onchain activity and airdrop rewards
  5. Log those stats into a spreadsheet and continue to update it using these tools weekly (or whatever you have the time for)

Both tools provide insights across many networks, completed tasks vs pending ones, and personalized airdrop checklists.
