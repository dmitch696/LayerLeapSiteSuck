üåü Why Soneium?

Soneium is a Layer 2 blockchain built with the OP Stack and backed by Sony. It's optimized for scalability and ultra-low fees, making it ideal for on-chain interactions. Given the hype around its ecosystem and major backers, there's growing speculation that Soneium could reward early users with a retroactive airdrop.

Before beginning to farm Soneium, keep in mind that you are no longer an early user if you are starting now. Being rewarded may still be possible, but "badges" were given to many users. Badges were given for users who had a certain amount of transactions before a certain date and to users who used certain protocols and completed tasks.

It may not be too late to farm Soneium, but unless you started farming it earlier and are looking to continue farming it and stand out, it may be best to focus on other chains/protocols.
